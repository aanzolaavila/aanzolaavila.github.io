What I Learned From Hash Functions

2022-08-17 :: Alejandro Anzola-Ávila
#hash  #security  #cryptography  #computerscience 
During 2019-2 semester I had the opportunity to do a research exchange to the US, did some research on Blockchain technologies and took a course on Applied Cryptography. While I am not going to talk about Blockchain in here, I will talk about Cryptography, hash functions in particular. Before this, I had a very limited idea of a lot of the concepts that are required from hash functions. Before going there I only had the brief idea that a hash function is a one-way function.
